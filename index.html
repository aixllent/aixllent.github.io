Sustainability at Xllent AI™

Today, data center computing generates 2% of greenhouse gas emissions worldwide, comparable with the airline industry, and demand for computing in complex and data-hungry applications is accelerating it. However the shift to renewable sources of power is only in early stages. While hyperscale cloud providers like AWS, Google, Azure, and Oracle are setting goals and making strides to increase the efficiency in their operations, the industry needs new innovations in power delivery to ensure organizations have access to highly available and cost-effective sustainable computing. Xllent AI is working with partners who specialize in providing publicly available computing that is 100% powered by renewable sources.

Industries We're Accelerating

Industry - Life Science

Genomics analysis, drug discovery, medical devices, and numerous other life science applications demand exceptionally high-performance computing to process complex data and drive innovation. By leveraging the Xllent.ai cloud platform, scientists gain access to virtually unlimited computational resources that enable advanced simulations, large-scale data analysis, and accelerated research workflows. With its state-of-the-art collaboration tools and efficient data transfer capabilities, Xllent.ai empowers researchers to seamlessly share insights, manage massive datasets, and push the boundaries of scientific discovery.

Industry - Aerospace

Aerospace HPC R&D engineering requires high performance computing to perform large-scale structural, fluid dynamics, thermal, and electromagnetic analyses. Xllent AI's customers leverage the latest cloud hardware technology to analyze complex applications including wing structural strength, turbine flow, antenna placement, and lightning strike.

Industry - EDA Semiconductor

Engineers are utilizing big compute on Xllent AI to develop the very hardware and systems that power the cloud. From IC and PCB design and analysis, to interconnect, cooling equipment and power storage. With natively integrated DOE and optimization tools, electrical engineers can route multi-layer boards, design complex systems on chip (SOCs), run complex thermal analyses, ensure power delivery, or minimize emissions, EMC noise margins and crosstalk.

Industry - Automotive

From optimizing the vehicle aerodynamics to efficiency analysis for combustion and electrification, to crash safety simulations, automotive engineers run an expansive range of computer-aided engineering (CAE) simulations on Xllent AI's platforms to build new cars faster.
